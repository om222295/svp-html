Narendra Modi

About

Narendra Modi is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India, serving since 2014. He is a leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

Early Life

He was born on 17th September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat. Before entering politics, he worked in the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS).

Achievements

  • Launched Swachh Bharat Abhiyan
  • Promoted Digital India campaign
  • Improved infrastructure and foreign relations
